Description

Introduction (10 minutes):

Intro to photobooks:

  • Ask trainees if they like taking pictures of their holidays, family occasions etc.
  • Ask them how they store them to look at them later. How often do they then actually look at them?
  • Create a link to our photobooks:
  • Our customers are just like you and I. We take photos as memories and later often just throw them in a box or store them on a computer.....
  • Wouldn´t it be so much nicer to have them bound in a book with good quality printing? We could just grab it off our bookshelf whenever we want to reminisce about the good times we had on our last holiday, family party etc.
  • Vistaprint photobooks allow our customers to do exactly that!


Website Navigation Activity:

Familiarize yourself with our Photobook offering online. Spend approx. 15 minutes to briefly find out about sizes, colors, additional options, delivery, customer feedback FAQ.


Prep Instructions:

  • Trainers should open the "Photobook PGI Activity" PDF.
  • Divide class in teams/groups.
  • Print questions as per group.
  • Cut out questions (all teams will get the same questions).
  • Place in cups.
  • The first team to complete the activity will earn 10 points. For each correct answer, they'll earn 5 points and for each incorrect answer, the team loses 2 points.
  • Run activity for 25 minutes.
