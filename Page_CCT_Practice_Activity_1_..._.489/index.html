After the NHs finished the first CCT practice activity bring them together and first ask:

  • Which functions did they find handy/useful?
  • Did they have challenges during the assignment?
  • How did they overcome the challenges?
  • What best practices could some of them use to make designing quicker/easier?
  • Select three new hires to display their design

The last question will probably lead to a discussion about some functions of the Studio/CCT. Here are a few things you can cover:

  • How the Smart Guides helps the Specialist and customer to avoid mistakes and help to align text
  • Talk about the safety margins and why we have them. Emphasize that text needs to be within the safety margin whereas background colors/shapes should cover the whole area incl. margins
  • (if not done before) Show shapes like rectangles, circles and stars and how NHs can use them
  • Show the group function by marking all objects you want to group with shift. Then swipe over the marked objects with the mouse 
  • Explain CRTL+A shortcut to select all objects of a side. With this, it is easily possible to copy the front to the backside
  • Tell how they can zoom in and zoom out (CRTL +/-)
  • Show additional short cuts like copy and paste or add  short cuts which you know it makes the workflow better
