Description



Scenario

Imagine! Ms. Johnson is calling you. She wants to promote her dog grooming salon and has planned a small campaign for which she needs the postcards she designed with her logo and an offer. Unfortunately, now she is calling with a complaint: her package has not arrived and the delivery due date was yesterday.

Of course you empathize with her and together you figure out that the best solution for her is to receive a reorder: she still wants her postcards and she needs them by the end of next week latest. She is happy with that solution.

Did you realize that before you send off the reorder, there's an additional step to take?
You want to check her design to make sure she gets the perfect postcard to promote her business.

Steps

  1. Click this link to open her design
  2. Click the Order My Own button and save the design to your own account (log in if necessary)
  3. Save the design to your own account and then, you can switch to your own locale if you need to
  4. After saving, you can switch to your market's Vistaprint website
  5. Take any design edits necessary to improve the file
  6. Be prepared to show your design in class and "sell" the changes you made to your customer

Finishing up
  1. Order a PDF proof of your design and save it to your PC.
  2. Submit the Proof you created for your design below. 
    Note: if you've chosen a template with special finishes, a PDF proof is not available. In that case, please upload a screen shot.
  3. Click the Submit all and finish button to submit your work. This is necessary in order for the activity to be marked as complete.
