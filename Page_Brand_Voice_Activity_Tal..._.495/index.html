Email 1 - Placing my order

Talking points

  • Cut off time (maybe repetition after KB Scavenger Hunt but that’s not bad): if the order is placed on Friday, Saturday or Sunday and the 3-day shipping option is chosen, the package will arrive latest Wednesday
    • TRUST our shipping options, if we encourage the customer to “to be sure” choose the 2-day option.
    • Remember that it’s not only placing the order by 6pm EST, also the payment needs to be in
    • Remember that it’s LATEST Wednesday, package may still arrive on Tuesday or even Monday, so that should be mentioned, otherwise the customer might take a day off to wait for the package on Wednesday.
  • Did you mention something about the conference?
  • Did you mention something about the draft of the business card, i.e. did you quickly check the design?
  • Did you add a link to the shipping options in our FAQ section, so she can always click and get the latest information?
  • How did you address the customer, Ms. Jones/Sylvia? Formal or rather informal?

Email 2 - Tickets?

Talking points

  • Did anyone show empathy? How did you do that?
  • What product did you recommend?
  • How did you present the offer?
  • Was the product relevant?
  • Did you mention anything about the event?

Email 3 - Tennis ball

Talking points

  • Did anyone show empathy?

  • Did you encourage the customer to regularly check our website because we might start offering them in the future?
