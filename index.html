Me as a Customer

In order to prepare you for your role as a CARE Specialist and also assisting our customers, it's important that you first experience what it is like to be a customer at Vistaprint.

So ask yourself:
  • Do I know how to create a Vistaprint account using my Vistaprint email address and password?
  • How do I edit my Vistaprint account settings based on my needs and interests?
  • How can I navigate the Vistaprint website by locating, selecting and customizing a template using the Studio?
  • Do I know how to order products I have designed by selecting quantities, additional options (such as paper stock and add on items), shipping speed and payment option?
  • How can I use the Help Section to contact the CARE Center as well as locate answers to some of the Frequently Asked Questions?
  • How do I interpret the Terms of Use and determine how they govern my access or use of the Vistaprint site?

Me as a Specialist

In the Me as a Specialist module, we will be reviewing internal Vistaprint tools, processes and best practices when resolving a customer issue or query.

This training module has been designed to help you:

  • Determine the customer’s needs and how to resolve their queries using the contact processes (PGI, AWO, AWD, SOAP) as a guideline.
  • Navigate the CAREConnect tool in order to locate, edit and document a customer's account.
  • Use the CAREKB to locate internal information in order to best determine the right course of action.
  • Use the Advanced Studio and Layers to make edits to a customer's design based on the customer’s need/ issue being faced.
  • Locate, apply and remove Promo codes from a customer’s account.
  • Determine if a promotion was used on an order that the customer placed by using the Search Query Tool.
  • Apply the brand voice when communicating with customers; increasing customer loyalty and reducing customer effort.

    Customized Workshops

    The Customized Workshop module is a core part of your overall 90 days Onboarding Plan. Here you will find training modules and activities that will give you the additional knowledge and skills to become a well-rounded CARE Specialist, who is able to assist customers with more advanced queries. 



    This training module has been designed to help you:

    • Determine how to assist customers who are victims or are suspected of committing Fraud.
    • Use the GIMP tool to make advanced design edits such as removing the background from an image.
    • Increase customer loyalty and reduce customer effort by closing Soft Skills./ Customer Service skill gaps.
    • Improve your designing skills by applying knowledge of Print and Design Basics when designing or making recommendations.
    • Assist customers with general Digital queries and designing their website using SiteBuilder 1, 2 and 3.
