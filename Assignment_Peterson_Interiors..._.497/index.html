Description

Scenario

Janet Peterson calls you. She is an interior designer. She has been ordering her business cards with us for a few years now and her business is growing. She asks if we have a solution for the paperwork she hands over to her clients after they had an exploration meeting with her. 

You thought of our presentation folder and shared your idea with her. She is really enthusiastic about it and her budget allows trying it out. She asks you if you can help her create one that fits the design of her business card. It doesn't have to be exactly as the business card, but the presentation folder should match.

In addition, you two come up with the idea of putting a postcard in the presentation folder, along with the paperwork. This way, her clients can share the postcard with anyone they know that would also want to redesign their house, so she can bring great design home to as much people as possible.

You offer to create a postcard for her as well, that matches the design of the presentation folder and the business card.
