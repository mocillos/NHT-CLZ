Description

Creating a Vistaprint Account on Vptest.

Page content

Introduction: 

  • In this activity, you will be creating an account on Vistaprint’s internal test website (Vptest). 
  • Follow the steps outlined below to complete this task. 
  • Once you have created your account, please return to the CARE Learning Zone.



Time to complete activity:

7 mins


Steps:

  1. Go to www.vptest.com 
  2. Click My Account > Sign In at the top right of the page.
  3. Type your email address using this format username_us@tst.vistaprint.net
  4. Select the No, I am a new customer option.
  5. Complete the remaining fields.
    • Fields marked with an asterisk are required.
  6. Ensure the information you entered are correct and click the Sign Up button.
