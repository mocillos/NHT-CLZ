Description

Introduction: 

  • In this activity, you will be assisting resetting your Vistaprint password, using the Password reset option on the Vistaprint site. 
  • Follow the steps outlined below to complete this task. 


Time to complete activity:

9 mins


Steps:

  1. Go to www.vistaprint.com.
  2. Click on My account.
  3. On the Sign In page, click Reset my password.
  4. Type the email address associated with your Vistaprint account.
  5. Click Submit.
  6. Click OK in the pop-up box.
  7. Go to your email inbox and locate the Password reset email from Vistaprint. 
    • Please note that this may take some time.
  8. Click the Reset Password link.
  9. On the Password Assistance page, fill out necessary fields.
  10. Click Submit.


Note:

  • The Vistaprint account password reset process is intended to provide guidance regarding password reset requests. 
  • This process should be followed by all Customer Care Specialists.
