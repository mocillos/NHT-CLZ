Description



Your Situation

Together with your co-founders you have already created your small business. You have also thought about your logo, business concept, slogan, unique selling point(s) and more. Recently, you heard that there will be a fair for small businesses in your town. This is a great opportunity you can't refuse because this is perfect to present yourself in the market.

You have just started your business and your budget is limited. You can spend 100 dollars per team member. Even if this seems to be a small amount of money, you have heard about Vistaprint offering high-quality print products at affordable prices. This can be very helpful.


The Assignment

Before going to the fair:

  • Discuss with your co-founders what goals you have.
  • Ask yourself, what is it that you would want to accomplish?
  • Make your goal(s) specific and quantifiable! Don't say: I want to be successful. Say something like: At the end of the show I want x number of...
Take some time for discussion. 15-20 minutes should work.

After you have committed to your goal(s), go to:

  • www.vptest.com and login
  • Start to create your products
  • Each team member is required to make use of his/her budget of 100 dollars as best as possible. 
  • Create three products. At least one product must be made of paper. You need to select  one of our TOP PRODUCTS (Business cards, T-shirts, Flyers, Postcards, Brochures, Banners and Invitations) and any other from Customer Flyer.
  • Discuss with your team member(s) who will create which product(s).
  • Please don't use products from the Promotique-website (the Promotional Products tab).
  • Take a screenshot of the products created and upload it to the CLZ.

For designing, you'll have 60 minutes.

Be ready to present your goals and your products to the class afterwards. Have fun!
