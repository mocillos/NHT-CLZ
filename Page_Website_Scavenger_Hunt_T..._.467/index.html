Q1 :  Earlier today, you ordered 500 wedding invitations on the Vistaprint website. However, you notice that you have chosen the wrong paper stock. How can you change the order?

&

Q2: As a customer, you would like to cancel an order because you wrote the wrong telephone number on your product. How can you cancel the order?

A1 & A2:

  • Good starting point to talk about that we always have solutions if anything unwanted happens from the customer site
  • In both situations, the customer can go to Order History and either cancel the order or change the design
  •  Possibility for a trainer to go to the Order History to show where a customer could change the order or to cancel in his/her account
  • The option to change the design will appear below the image of the product.
  • Both options are available until we start printing the products. After this point, the option to cancel or edit the design is not clickable any longer.
  • Then the customer can easily call or write us so that we can fix it for him
  • Both topics are also immediately visible when open the help section because it is one of the most wanted actions customers want to do.
  • A pure cancellation will trigger a mail notification and the status in the Order History will show canceled.
  • For Design edits made it will also trigger cancellation, and will place a new order with the updated design. This will both trigger email cancellation and a new order email. 


Q3: You need to contact Vistaprint Customer Service regarding an order you recently placed. What are the various channels in which you can contact Vistaprint Customer Service?

A3: Phone, Email and Chat

  •  Offering multiple channels according to customer preferences
  • The displayed options are flexible, so the customer can see the currently available channels. If, for example, no specialist is assigned to chat then Ops can turn it off from the website
  • Speak about opening hours which is 24/7 in the Anglo-American markets and tell about the opening hours of other markets as well if it applies to your center

Q5: You own a computer shop and you are interested in a broader selection of clothes, keyboards, and mouses with your brand. Does Vistaprint offer these kinds of products? If so, where would you go to find them?

A5: Vistaprint does not offer it directly. It is offered by Promotique. This section is on the top left at the Vistaprint homepage and is called Promotional Products.

  • This question works as an introduction to Promotique
  •  Promotique offers a wider range of promotional marketing products
  • These products can be integrated to the Vistaprint Homepage as well over the time
  •  If the VP site doesn’t offer what the customer wants its maybe on the promotique page – it’s worth having a look at it.
  • Payment and delivery conditions are different from VP (10 days delivery, only credit card accepted)
  • Customer will call a different number since a dedicated team will care for questions regarding Promotique


Q6: As a longtime customer you have been very satisfied with our products. You like to refer Vistaprint to a friend of yours. Does Vistaprint offer such a program?

A6: Yes

  • Referring a friend is not available for all markets
  • It’s a method to get new customers and make customers loyal
  • Information can either be found in the help section and at the very bottom of the VP homepage
  • How it works: The customer has to fill out a short form, gets a link, shares it with a friend, and if this friend orders for at least the required $ amount (US= 40 USD, CA= 50 CAD) the customer gets a voucher (US = 20 USD, CA = 25 CAD) which is valid for one year (https://reward.vistaprint.com/Refer/Faqs?key=4c491a42-01fd-4730-8315-6c19af291763&culture=en-US)
    (https://reward.vistaprint.com/Refer/Faqs?key=4c491a42-01fd-4730-8315-6c19af291763&culture=en-CA)
  • The voucher code will be sent to the customer by email within 3 working days of their purchase.


Q7: You are ordering business cards at Vistaprint for the first time and notice that they offer Brilliant Finishes. However, you are not quite sure what that is. Where can you find more information about it and what kinds of Brilliant Finishes do we offer for this product?

A7: We offer metallic and spot gloss business cards

  • Ask the group what types of Brilliant Finishes we offer.
  •  Go to the help section > Designing my product > Brilliant Finishes and show the images/videos there. Here a customer can get an example how a Brilliant Finish looks like
  •  It’s great to create outstanding business cards emphasizing logos or text


Q8: It is 8:50 on a chilly Friday evening. You need to call Vistaprint for design help so that your order can be placed before the weekend. You are not sure if the customer service department is still open. Where can you locate this information on the site and is the Customer Care Center still open?

A8: For NA is open Sat/Sun: 8am-8PM. Mon-Fri: 7am- 3am

In other locations, it is different and is shown in the help section at the contact us box

  • We are always there for the customers and have generous opening hours especially in the main markets
  • The telephone number is a local one so that customers will have minimum costs no matter where and when they contact us.
  • We promise the customer to write back within 24 hours in case of emails.
  •  Opening hour decisions depend on customer preferences, competitor situation, and economic considerations (costs & benefits)
  • By clicking see all contact methods the customer can see more information like how busy the contact center is (depending on locale)


Q9: A customer ordered a T-Shirt and is not satisfied with the quality of the fabric. He was told about the Vistaprint satisfaction guarantee. What does it promise in detail and where can you find the information about it?

A9: If you ever have an issue with your Vistaprint order, it's our goal to resolve it to best meet your needs. So if a reprint or account credit doesn’t solve your problem, we’ll refund your money 100% (found at the web footer, click on  Every time. Any reason. Or we’ll make it right)

  • Say that the satisfaction guarantee is the core of our customer-centricity
  • It’s our commitment to keeping customers happy in the way that we solve issues with the lowest effort possible
  • We assume good intentions and believe the customer if he says that he is not happy or if things did not work well
  • The trainer can tell about the history that in the past we were more sales-driven than customer-centric so in case of a typo, the customer had to pay money again.
  • It makes lives easier for the customer and the specialist.


Q10:

You found an image on www.shutterstock.com that you really like. You saved it to your computer and uploaded it to your design.  Now you notice that there is a watermark on the image.

 Will the customer service Specialist delete the watermark from the image for you?

A10: The customer service Specialist will not do this due to copyright issues

  • We don’t erase watermarks to protect us and the customer from copyright argues
  • Also, the design services don’t erase watermarks
  • Point to our image gallery and templates. We have a lot of images the customer can use
  • If the customer can’t find a suitable image he can use images without copyright called creative commons or sites like pixarbay.com


Q11: You created a Vistaprint account three weeks ago, and since then you have received over 15 emails from Vistaprint. You are frustrated with all the “spam” and want to opt-out of promotional emails and offers. How long can it take for these emails to stop?

A11: It can take up to 10 days for email communications to stop. Also, you will always receive order confirmation emails, regardless of your communication preferences.


  • Point out that the amount of time it takes to opt-out might be connected to campaigns that have already been rolled out
  • Still, we have the experience that it usually goes faster than 10 days
  • Reminder emails and order confirmations do not belong to marketing emails and can’t be opt-out.


Q12: You are interested in ordering horizontal Flyers in the size of 5.5 “ x 8.5 “ You want glossy paper with premium quality. What is the cost of 50 Flyers?

A12: Here you must check with the group and show it in one example.

  • It's easy for the customer to get a price from a product he is interested in
  • This was learning from the past where customers complain about not being transparent
  • That’s why the customer can see the price once they select the materials
  • Usually, the price is shown in black color. That’s the normal price
  • Red prices will indicate an applied promotion
  • Adding upgrades like changing paper stock, adding backside printing, or adding accessories will affect the total price of the product.
