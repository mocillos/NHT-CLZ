Description

Share the following points:

  • We have been asked to find a savings of $3.5MM in the cost of credits. 
  • There are three main areas we are looking to target to achieve these savings:
    1. Changing our DIFY pricing model - keeping the full $5 fee and stopping the practice of converting the fee into a Merchandise Credit (we are currently testing this first to make sure it is a sound business move).
    2. Improving the quality of Vistaprint products and delivery performance (carrier performance in EU seems to be particularly problematic and a good opportunity for savings).
    3. Addressing areas where we may be exceeding the intent of our “Absolutely Guaranteed” satisfaction policy (e.g. giving customers red sale prices during non-red sale weeks).
