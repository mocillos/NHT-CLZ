Description

Scenario

Imagine! Melissa Starr is calling you. She shows you the design of her business card:


She has been using that design for over a decade now and thinks it's time for something more modern, more appealing. Something that reflects her experience as a jeweler and her cheerful personality that will draw potential customers' attention. One unique selling point she mentioned to you, is that she offers a free one hour consultation for people that want to have a one-of-a-kind design created for them.

Steps

  1. Go into the CCT as explained by your trainer
  2. Choose either a blank business card design or see if you can find a great template for Ms. Starr
  3. Design her business card
    Note: Be creative! The business card should fit the customer's needs and does not have to be a copy of the old design. Also, you are free to create a back to the business card, if you want to.
Finishing up
  1. Take a screenshot of your design and save it to your PC.
  2. Upload the screenshot for your design below.
  3. Click the Submit all and finish button to submit your work. This is necessary in order for the activity to be marked as complete.
