Page content

Always ask

  • Did anyone encounter problems when designing?
  • Was there anything you wanted to put in but couldn't manage?

Discuss the troubles encountered and ask for input from the group before providing the solution.

Talking topics

  • The safety margin - check while NH are designing if anyone is making safety margin errors and show on the big screen to explain:
    • text should be well inside
    • logo should be well inside
    • background color/image should should extend to the full bleed
    • click the safety-margin info box (top left) in the Studio to help explain to the customer
  • Working with layers
    • Address questions about layers

Additional exercise

The customer loves the business card you created but she would like to have it on a different type business card.

  • Demo to your New Hires how one can copy a design to a different type of product
  • Ask them to try it out on their computers
