Description

Introduction: 

  • In this activity, you will recreate the business card design below in CCT using the VistaStudio.
  • Follow the steps outlined below to complete this task. 


Time to complete activity:

60 mins

Steps:

  1. Click on the following link to access CAREForce: https://vpcare.my.salesforce.com/console
    • DO NOT CHANGE YOUR AUX STATE.
  2. Click the Identify Customer link.
  3. Search for your account by entering your Vistaprint email address in the Email field.
  4. Click the Search button.
  5. Select the Offline Category.
  6. Click the Open in CAREForce link .
  7. Click on New Order in the Right Panel.
  8. CCT will open in new a tab. Enter your credentials with your cc_username and your CCT password to proceed.
  9. You will then be taken to the CCT version of the Vistaprint website where you can now select a business card template.
  10. Customize the template so that it looks exactly as the business card shown below.
    • You must use the same images, colors, shapes, information and font.
  11. Make sure you save the design to your account.
  12. Once you have completed designing the business card, screenshot your design and save it to your PC.
  13. Upload your screenshot to the CARE Learning Zone. 
  14. Click the Submit all and finish button to submit your work. This is necessary in order for the activity to be marked as complete.

Notes:

  • We will be reviewing your account to verify that the Business Card was recreated. 
  • In addition, once you complete this activity, your Trainer will be quizzing you. So pay special attention to the differences you noticed between the Studio in CCT and designing a product on the website.
