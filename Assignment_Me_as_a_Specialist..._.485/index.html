Description

Introduction: 

  • This Scavenger Hunt practice activity is designed to help new employees navigate the CAREKB and locate information on Vistaprint’s products, processes and services.
  • Follow the steps outlined below to complete this task. 

Resources Needed: 

PC

Time to complete activity:

45 mins

Steps to Access the Scavenger Hunt:

  1. Click on the PDF file below to get started.
  2. PDF File will automatically download and will display in the download bar at the bottom of your screen.
  3. Click on the arrow beside the file and select Always open in Adobe Reader option.
    • If the file is not opened in Adobe Acrobat Reader, you will be unable to save your answers.
  4. Locate the answers to the Scavenger Hunt activity by going to en.carekb.vistaprint.net.
  5. Once you have located an answer, go the PDF file and enter the information in respective field provided.
    • Please note that there is a 200 character limit for each answer field.

Steps to Submit Your Answers:

  1. Once you have finished the Scavenger Hunt activity, save your PDF file to your PC.
  2. Return to the CARE Learning Zone and click on the Add Submission button.
  3. You can drag and drop the file in the space provided or upload the PDF file.
  4. Click the Save changes button. This is necessary in order for the activity to be marked as complete.
  5. In addition, please attach the updated PDF and email it to your assigned peer.

Note:

  • Once you complete this activity, your Trainer will be quizzing you. So pay special attention to where you located your answers.
  • This is practice activity will not be graded and does not impact your overall course performance or score. 
  • Peer & Self Reviewing is employed to monitor your progress throughout the course, and help you and your trainer identify the areas where you need greater support.
