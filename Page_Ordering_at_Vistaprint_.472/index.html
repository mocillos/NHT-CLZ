Ordering Products designed in Vptest

Page content

Introduction: 

  • In this activity, you will be ordering the Products and the logo you designed on www.vptest.com
  • Follow the steps outlined below to complete this task. 
  • Once you have placed your order, please return to the CARE Learning Zone.


Time to complete activity:

30 mins

Steps:

  1. Go to www.vptest.com and log into your account.
  2. Click on My Account in the upper right hand corner of the page.
  3. Select the My Portfolio option
  4. Add each product you designed to the Shopping Cart by clicking the Order link.
    • Select the quantities and options that would best meet your needs.
    • Select any accessories or matching products that are suitable for you.
  5. Click the Checkout button to continue.
  6. Use the Shipping and Billing Address that your trainer provides.
    • 275 Wyman Street
    • Waltham, MA 02451
  7. Select Credit Card as your payment option and use the following info:
    • Credit Card Type:   Visa
    • Credit Card Number: 4111111111111111
    • Expiration: 12/ 2020
    • CVV/ Security Code: 111
  8. Click the Place Order button.
