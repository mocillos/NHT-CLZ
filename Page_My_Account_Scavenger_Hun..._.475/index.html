Q1: Name the ways with which a customer can locate/ find his account number!

A1: Hover over my account and if you click on my account it is on top left

  • Account number being essential for us and the customers if they contact us
  • The easiest way is to hover over my account to reduce effort to the minimum to access the most important functions
  • Some people have the habit to click and it takes them to the more detailed account section. Still, the account number is orange so that customer can easily see it.


Q2: Do you have the option to change your billing and/or shipping address in the Account Update section of My Account?

A2: This option is not available in my account but in the order process

  • People often assume they can change the addresses in the account section but at Vistaprint changing addresses belongs to the order process. It’s not an account related process.
  • Still they can change and save their addresses when ordering
  • Having it in the order process the advantage is that customers are reminded to probably change their addresses if needed.


Q3: Can you upload an image or a logo directly to the account without selecting a product/template? If so, how?

A3: Yes it's possible in my account > My Images and Logos. They have to create a folder and then upload pictures to it.

  • Advantage for the customer is a centralized place to manage the images
  • Images are saved for 90 days if not ordered
  • For the Specialist it can be a quicker way to let customer upload a design or a photo in case of a complaint
  • Maximum size of image is 38 MB


Q4: What are the differences between the Order History and My Portfolio/My Projects?

A4: Order History shows just the orders the customer placed. My Portfolio contains items the customer has created and either ordered or not ordered.

  • Show the differences between Order History and My Portfolio
  • My Portfolio explaining and showing: Edit, Rename, Copy, delete a Design, green mark if design was ordered and what it means (no deletion possible), share button with facebook
  • Explaining My order history: quick reorder, order details, order ID, what you can see when clicking the order ID, tracking number, resend confirmation mail etc.


Q5: How can you delete a stored payment method from your account?

A5: Hover over my account, click on Stored Payments and then select delete next to the payment information to removed.

·         As Specialist it can be helpful to delete the stored payment method in case of issues and try again applying the payment method


Q6: By choosing a design template (example: Standard business card) you will have the possibility to mark designs with a heart. Why do you think a customer does this and where can he/she find the list of marked designs?

A6: To save a design for later. Go to My Account and then select My Favorites.

  • it can be practical for the customer being able to preselect designs, especially when there are lots of designs like we offer with business cards.
  • another reason can be for individual events like birthdays and weddings where couples can mark designs and show it to their partners before they order.
  • Maybe NHs will come up with more ideas for use.

Q8: Name all the options you can use to update your marketing preferences in your account settings and describe what choices you have.

A8:

1.Yes, I am interested in receiving e-mails about new products, services, and promotional offers.

2. Yes! Sign me up to receive special offers from carefully selected companies. Vistaprint may make my email address available to these companies.

3. Yes, occasionally make my mailing address and phone number available to other reputable organizations whose special offers, products, or services I might find interesting.

Talking points:

  • Emphasize that the customer has full control over the marketing mails and how data is shared with 3rd parties
  • Emphasize that reminder mails regarding a product in the cart and products in my portfolio are not affected by the settings here and cannot be opt-out


Q9 Where can customers manage their digital products and services? Name the available options of this section!

A9: Its all you can find at  My Digital Marketing/My Services if you click on My Account.

  •  This can serve as a little introduction to what digital products we offer and that this is the spot where the customer has access to the settings; more details will come later in the digital trainings
  • The target is not to click and explain every point here, its just about that NHs have seen the options.
  • It can be considered to introduce the Design Service we offer for some countries as well


Q10: In what way does the “save a copy” option in My Portfolio/Projects can help the customer? Think of a few scenarios!

A10:

  • It’s useful to create multiple versions of the same template with smaller changes like names.
  • Companies who want to order the same BCs with different names
  • This function is needed to prevent overwriting current versions.
  •  Then the customer can put all versions one by one to the cart and can place one order instead of single orders in which he would have to pay the delivery costs


Q11: Can I delete an item I have ordered? If yes, how and if no, can you explain why?

A11: No this is not possible.

  • We keep items which has been ordered due to legal reasons
  • Tax offices often wants to have data from the past and we have to keep this data
  •  For the customer the benefit is that he simply can reorder items quickly


Q12: What can you find in the topic “ideas and advices”?

A12: You find several tips on how to improve the design and the marketing effects of the products

  • Point out that we want to engage the Specialists to be advisors for the customer and here the customer and Specialist have a good point to start with. Since there are a lot of marketing basics explained. It’s a good page for the customers and Specialists as well.
