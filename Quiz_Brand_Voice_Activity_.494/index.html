Introduction

As a CARE Specialist, you will deal with many different requests from our customers. It is very important to hit the right tone when communicating with them and to provide them with relevant and complete information.
Before we introduce you to best practices, let's try providing general information by answering 3 customer emails.

Steps

Click on the Attempt Quiz Now button below to get started.

  1. Respond to the customer emails in the field provided, keeping in mind that
    • customers like to see correct punctuation, grammar and spelling
    • you may use the Vistaprint website to ensure you provide accurate information
  2. When you're done, hit the Next button.
  3. Click the Submit all and finish button to submit your answers. This is necessary in order for the activity to be marked as complete.

Once everyone has submitted their answers, we will discuss the outcomes!
