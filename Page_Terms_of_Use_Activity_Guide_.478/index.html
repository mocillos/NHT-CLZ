Introduce activity (2 mins):

  • The Terms of Use outline what the Vistaprint’s policies are regarding the access and use of the website.
  • Each of you will be assigned a section of the Vistaprint Terms of Use to review. 
  • If there are more than 10 persons in the class, then you may be paired.
  • Write a summary of the section you just read in 50 words or less
  • You will have 10 mins to complete this activity.


Conduct Terms of Use Presentations (38 mins)

  • Present the terms of use PowerPoint


Debrief (15 mins):

  • What challenges did you experience?
  • What interesting/ new thing did you learn?


Highlight (5 mins):

  • The Terms of Use states that Vistaprint isn’t responsible for errors created by the customer. However, we still need to be empathetic and help to resolve the issue.
  • Under the Use of Site section, it clearly states that Vistaprint has the right to copy, modify, create derivative works and vectorize any uploaded content for the purpose of fulfilling the customer’s order. This should not be confused with how we personalize marketing and promotional offers specifically to the customer.
  • Customers may receive marketing and promotional offers that are personalized with an image the customer uploaded on a product. 
  • This approach is similar to product suggestions made during the MPSO which include an example of what a product will look like using the customer’s image.
  • The promotional emails a customer receives are pulled directly from their Vistaprint account and are sent to them alone.
