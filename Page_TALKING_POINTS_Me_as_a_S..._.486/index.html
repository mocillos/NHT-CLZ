Q1:  Is a price list included in the Sample Kits package? Depending on the answer can you tell why we include or not include a price list?

Answer: No, Article: Sample Kits Overview

  • Prices can change permanently
  • We want customers to check prices on website
  • When giving prices via email, its better to send link to the product with the latest price
  • When giving prices via phone, better to name the black font price without discount

Q2: What is the local shipping cut-off time for a UK order being printed in the Venlo plant?

Answer: 8pm GMT, Name of Article: Shipping cut off times

  • We also need to have the payment before midnight
  • Advise the customer to better calculate some extra minutes in advance for payment

Q3: A customer placed an order on December 8th, 2018, with 3 Business Day shipping before the shipping cut off time. When is the latest date he is expected to receive his order?

Answer: Wednesday, 12th December, Name of Article: Shipping Speed Calculations

  • Cut off time for Weekend is Sunday Midnight
  • Doesn't matter if customer places order on Friday, Saturday or Sunday
  • Weekend days are not considered as business days (including holidays)
  • The last day of delivery is not the ultimate delivery day (no need for customers to take a day off for that)
  • Items can also come sooner either completely or in separate packages

Q4: Give two examples of content that are considered illegal.

Answer: Hate symbols, child abuse, child pornography content.  Article: Illegal and Inappropriate Content

  • First, help the customer as you would do with anyone else, don't discuss or "play" the lawyer
  • Flag it to the supervisor after the contact
  • Most people wanting to print illegal content know it and usually will not contact us
  • In Germany, swastika symbols will go to the police and it will be detected in the plant

Q5: You answer a call that has come into the queue. However, there is no one on the other end of the line (like a ghost). What should you do?

Answer: Try to gain attention for 30 seconds, then announce that you will terminate the call and do so. Article: Hold technique.

  • Customer still might hear you, maybe its a technical issue from our site so don't talk to colleagues
  • Can also happen that the customer was in the queue for a long time and is in another room not hearing you

Q6:  How long will a document that has not been ordered, remain in "My Portfolio/Projects", before being deleted?

Answer: 60 Days Article: Automatic document deletion

  • Explain that not ordered designs are stored 30 days in the cart, 60 days in My designs and 90 days in My images
  • Explain that even if designs not in the cart they might be in My Portfolio
  • We delete unpaid pictures to make space for the servers
  • Paid pictures/design will not be deleted, exception: photo calender (187 days)
  • Customer will get several reminder mails which can't be opt out.
  • A customer can't delete ordered pictures but we as Specialists can

Q7: How can you apply a promo code to an order?

Answer: Shopping cart  > click on "Do you have a promotional code?" > a box will appear> Type the promo code and hit enter/ click apply.  Article: Applying Promo Codes

  • In the cart each discounted product is visible and from there the customer can see possible delivery discounts when he proceed

Q8: Which account information can be used when verifying if you are speaking with the account holder?

Answer: Caller's name Article: Account Verification

  • Always ask for the caller’s name with an open-ended question
  • Always ask for information, don't give information
  • Do not change any account details if the customer is logged in. Guide him/her to do it, especially with sensitive data like payment and email addresses

 

Q9: You are assisting a customer with editing his business card design and notice that he has uploaded the following image. Can Vistaprint print the card with this image?

Answer: Yes   Article: Image Copyright Policy

  • If the image cannot be mistaken as money (same size and shape) we print it
  • Same goes for official documents (as long as they can't be mistaken its ok)
  • Refer to watermarked pictures found on Google and that we will not print them

Q10: When should you request a customer to send samples of their product when they have complaints about quality? How would the customer submit it?

Answer: If the quality flaw is caused from production (blurry images, misprint, wrong colors, cutting issues) the customer is requested to send an image to QualityNA@vistaprint.com

Article: Product & Design Quality Complaints

  • It's important that you help the customer in any way (analyze and soap for the right solution)
  • The customer can help us in refining our product processes
  • Its not mandatory for the customer, its a request from our site

Q11: A customer asks you a question about one of our products. However, you cannot find any information about it on the website on the Product Details page or under the Reviews, Questions and Answers section. What should you do next to resolve the customer’s query?

Answer: You will submit an answer on behalf of the customer Article: Product Information Requests

  • It is important to include the customer’s email address so the customer will be notified when an answer is provided.
  • Do not add the customer’s name or email address without their consent

 

Q12: By browsing through the Knowledge Base you notice that an information of  an article is outdated. How can you have an influence on improving the article so that it will show the accurate information in the future?

Answer: On top and at the button of each article there is a "submit feedback" option

  • Maintaining the accuracy of the CAREKB involves all of us
